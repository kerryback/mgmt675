# Magnolia Oil & Gas (MGY) Q3 2019 Earnings Call Transcript
Source: Motley Fool, November 6, 2019

## Key Speakers
- Steve Chazen - CEO
- Chris Stavros - CFO
- Brian Corales - VP, Investor Relations

## Prepared Remarks

### Steve Chazen
Strategy of "generating moderate production growth while spending within 60% of our cash flow." Q3 production grew ~10% sequentially with declining capital spend. Built $68 million in cash after capex and buybacks.

Giddings: "Two wells produce more than 2,700 BOE per day in their first 30 days online, which included 1,700 barrels of oil per day and 6,400 Mcf per day gas." Three additional appraisal wells planned for Q4.

### Chris Stavros
- Adjusted net income: $16 million ($0.06 per diluted share)
- Operating cash flow: $173 million
- Production: 71,300 BOE/day (10% sequential increase)
- Oil: 54% of volumes
- Total cash operating costs: $9.96 per BOE
- Long-term debt: $390 million
- Cash on hand: $164 million

## Q&A Highlights
- Jeffrey Grampp (Northland): Acquisition activity. Chazen: Sellers maintain unrealistic valuations
- Brian Downey (Citi): Oil mix. Chazen: Expects similar to Q3
- Neal Dingmann (SunTrust/Wells): Giddings leasing. Hold 450,000 net acres of 600,000 gross, seeking strategic additions
