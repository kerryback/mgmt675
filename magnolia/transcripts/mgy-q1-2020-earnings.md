# Magnolia Oil & Gas (MGY) Q1 2020 Earnings Call Transcript
Source: Motley Fool, May 12, 2020

## Key Speakers
- Steve Chazen - Chairman, President, and CEO
- Chris Stavros - EVP and CFO
- Brian Corales - VP, Investor Relations

## Prepared Remarks

### Steve Chazen
Outlined Magnolia's positioning during weak product prices:
- Cash position: $146 million cash, $450 million undrawn revolver
- Generated $23 million free cash flow in Q1
- Capital spending target: 60% of adjusted EBITDAX
- Cost reductions: Expect at least $55 million improvement in 2020 cash operating costs
- Giddings: Four new wells online in Q1 with average 60-day oil production rate of 800 bbl/d
- Well costs down more than 20% vs 2019 average in Giddings, despite ~25% longer laterals

### Chris Stavros
- Q1 cash flow from operations: $135 million
- Free cash flow: $23 million
- Total production: 68,400 BOE/day, up 10%
- Debt: $400 million, not maturing until 2026
- Cost savings target: ~$55 million in 2020
- Q2 production guidance: 62,000-65,000 BOE/day

## Q&A Highlights
- Analysts questioned completion timing, gas economics, cost trends, M&A strategy, and production shut-in decisions
- Chazen emphasized disciplined capital allocation tied to oil prices around $30/barrel for viable economics
- Potential gas development at $3/mcf gas prices
